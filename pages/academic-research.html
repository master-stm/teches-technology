Academic Research

At TechES, we pride ourselves on contributing to cutting-edge academic research. Our members collaborate with faculty advisors and industry professionals to pursue research in various fields of technology and engineering. Through our research initiatives, we aim to push the boundaries of knowledge and create innovative solutions to real-world problems.
